Baked Mac anc Cheese

Description

This baked mac and cheese is a quintessential comfort dish that combines tender elbow macaroni with a rich, velvety cheese sauce. The pasta is generously coated in a creamy blend of melted cheeses, creating a smooth and indulgent texture. Once baked, the top develops a perfectly crispy, golden-brown crust, adding a delightful crunch to each bite. The result is a warm and satisfying dish that balances gooey, cheesy goodness with a savory, toasted topping, making it a truly comforting and flavorful meal.

Recipe

  • 2 cups elbow macaroni
  • 2 tablespoons unsalted butter
  • 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 2 cups whole milk
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 2 cups shredded sharp cheddar cheese
  • 1 cup shredded Gruyère cheese
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1/4 teaspoon paprika
  • 1/4 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/2 cup breadcrumbs
  • 2 tablespoons melted butter
  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook the elbow macaroni in a large pot of salted boiling water according to package instructions until al dente. Drain and set aside.
  3. In a large saucepan, melt 2 tablespoons of butter over medium heat.
  4. Stir in the flour and cook for about 1 minute, until it forms a paste (roux).
  5. Gradually whisk in the milk and heavy cream, continuing to whisk until the mixture is smooth and starts to thicken, about 5 minutes.
  6. Reduce heat to low and stir in the shredded cheeses, salt, pepper, paprika, and garlic powder. Continue stirring until the cheese is completely melted and the sauce is smooth.
  7. Add the cooked macaroni to the cheese sauce, stirring until well-coated.
  8. In a small bowl, combine the breadcrumbs with 2 tablespoons of melted butter. This mixture will create a crispy topping.
  9. Pour the macaroni and cheese mixture into a greased baking dish.
  10. Sprinkle the breadcrumb mixture evenly over the top.
  11. Bake in the preheated oven for 25-30 minutes, or until the top is golden brown and the cheese is bubbling.
  12. Let the baked mac and cheese cool for a few minutes before serving. Enjoy your creamy, cheesy comfort dish!
